# CScottPassionProject

Elevator Pitch

This application is a memorial and support platform designed for parents who have lost a child. It allows users to share stories, upload photos, and express their emotions in a safe space while receiving support from others who understand their experience.

Problem Statement

Parents who have lost a child often struggle to find a safe and understanding space to express their grief. Traditional social media platforms can feel overwhelming, impersonal, or inappropriate for deeply emotional experiences, and many existing support options lack accessibility or privacy. There is a need for a dedicated digital space where grieving parents can share memories, process emotions, and connect with a supportive community that truly understands their loss.

Target User

The target user is a grieving parent/parents who might be looking for a safe, private, and supportive space to express their emotions and preserve the memory of their child.

Why This Project

This project is important to me because I want to build something that goes beyond functionality and truly helps people. Creating a space where individuals can feel heard, supported, and understood during one of the most difficult experiences in life is meaningful to me. Also being a parent that has lost a child, this app would've been very useful to me in my time of need.
